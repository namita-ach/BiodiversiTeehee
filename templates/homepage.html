The Biodiversity Tracking Database is a versatile, web-based platform designed to support researchers, conservationists, and urban planners in managing biodiversity data within specific regions.

It streamlines species cataloging, habitat tracking, and conservation monitoring through user-friendly data entry, visualization tools, and geographic mapping features.

Accessible on both web and mobile devices, the database also fosters community involvement by allowing citizen scientists to contribute.

This centralized tool enhances understanding and preservation of local ecosystems, supporting informed decisions and effective conservation efforts.

Species

Records essential information about various species, including identification, habitat, and conservation status.

Sustainability Inititatives

Captures details of projects aimed at promoting sustainable practices and biodiversity conservation.

Biodiversity Metric

Tracks specific biodiversity metrics related to species count and health within different initiatives.

User

Manages user authentication, roles, and contact information for system participants.

Report

Stores generated reports with analysis and insights on species trends and initiative effectiveness.
